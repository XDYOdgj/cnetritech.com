10ft x 8ft IFR Polyester Backdrop Curtains

Elevate the ambiance of any event space with these versatile and stylish backdrop curtains. Crafted from high-quality 100% polyester, they are built to last and retain their elegant look through frequent use. Each curtain is 5ft x 8ft, and together they form a 10ft wide backdrop, creating a stunning visual for weddings, birthdays, and celebrations.

These event drapes are not only visually appealing but also safe to use. They are IFR certified, ensuring they meet the highest standards for indoor flame retardancy and providing peace of mind during any gathering. The 4-inch rod pocket on top makes installation effortless, and they are perfect for use in a pipe and drape system.

The semi-opaque design of these rod pocket curtains adds a subtle yet refined touch to your backdrop, making it ideal for both professional and personal settings. Their stain and wrinkle-resistant properties ensure that cleaning is simple, and they can withstand a variety of cleaning solutions without compromising their quality or appearance.
